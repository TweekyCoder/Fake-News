How To Identify?
Identification
Though it looks hard, but there are many ways of identifying fake/fraud news. You just need to be extra attentive and these 10 basic rules will help you to identify them.
The 10 Steps Method
  1. A Visual Assessment - Check for any spelling mistakes, check pictures to find any clues about where its taking place.
  2. Check the source - Is the publisher trusted? Is the news website trusted? have they been caught for fake news before?
  3. Check the website URL - Is it a copy of a major news publisher? Example: www.WASHINGTONPOST.com could be: www.WASHINGT0NPOST.com
  4. Check The About section - Who is the actual creator? Is it a copy, is something different or odd? This is a major clue.
  5. Who is the publisher/author? - What is the auhtor's other articles? Is the author trustworthy?
  6. What is the key point? - What is the author trying to state? Is this point true/authentic?
  7. Spelling, Punctuation, Grammar, - Has the author purposely misspelt a word? Has it been edited?
  8. See Quotes - Who are these people? What have they said? Did they actually state this?
  9. See other newswriters - Have they written this? If other sources havent, this is likely to be false.
  10. Go to A fact checker - factcheck.org, snopes.com, politifact.com - see other fact checking websites!
